Unlocking Growth Opportunities for Wellness Tech Company through Smart Device Data Analysis: A Bellabeat Case Study

Goal: The goal of this case study is to analyze smart device data to identify new growth opportunities for Bellabeat, a wellness technology company that develops health-focused smart products for women.

Objective: The objective of the marketing analytics team at Bellabeat is to gain insights into consumer behavior by following a comprehensive data analysis process, which includes asking the right questions, preparing relevant data, processing it using appropriate tools, analyzing the data to identify trends and relationships, sharing findings through visualizations, and presenting final conclusions and recommended approaches to inform Bellabeat’s marketing strategy.

Approach: By collecting data on activity, sleep, stress, and reproductive health, the company empowers women with knowledge about their own health and habits. The marketing analytics team will focus on analyzing smart device usage data for a Bellabeat product and providing high-level recommendations for how these trends can inform Bellabeat’s marketing strategy.
